1. Getting Started

Install it on your phone

The Control Room is a Progressive Web App — add it to your home screen so reminders reach you:

  1. 1iPhone (Safari): tap Share → Add to Home Screen.
  2. 2Android (Chrome): menu → Add to Home screen / Install app.
  3. 3When prompted, allow notifications — this is how daily task reminders reach you.

First sign-in

  1. 1Open the login link you were sent and sign in with your temporary password.
  2. 2You'll be asked to set a new password (complete-setup). Do it once — you won't be asked again.
  3. 3You land on the Dashboard. Your menu shows only what your role can reach.
Tip: The app is the source of truth for the cohort — if it isn't in the app, it didn't happen. Don't keep a side spreadsheet.

2. Roles & Access

There are three roles. Your role decides which pages you see and what you can edit.

RoleCan doCannot do
AdminEverything — all modules, settings, user management, cohorts, reviews & reminders config, import & export.—
FacilitatorView all data; action assignment reviews and sessions; see dashboards and alerts.Manage users, change global settings, run imports/exports.
Community ManagerSee every page (read-only on most); edit Participants and Community; work role tasks; flag risk; raise escalations.Edit modules outside Participants/Community; manage users or settings.
Note: Missing a page you think you should have? That's an access question for an admin — raise it rather than working around it.

3. The Dashboard

Your home screen and daily health check. It shows:

  • Today's attention — red-risk students and the review backlog that need eyes now.
  • Workload by owner — overdue work grouped by role. If your role shows overdue, clear it.
  • Escalations — surfaced for the core team so issues don't hide in DMs.

Your daily 5 minutes (CMs)

  1. 1Open Tasks. Anything overdue (amber) — do it or comment why it's blocked.
  2. 2Check Dashboard → Workload by owner. Clear your role's overdue items.
  3. 3Glance at Today's attention for red-risk students or review backlog.

4. Participants & Risk

The roster of everyone in the cohort. For each student you track attendance, submission status, risk level, owning CM, and next action. CM and Admin can edit here.

Update a student

  1. 1Open Participants and click the student's row.
  2. 2Update attendance and submission for the current week the same day it happens.
  3. 3Add a Next action so whoever picks it up knows the plan.

Flagging a student at risk

Set Risk = Red (or Amber) early — not at the end of the week.

Tip: Setting Risk = Red automatically creates an outreach task on the owning CM. Flagging is also how you trigger help — you don't have to chase it separately.

5. Assignment Reviews

The weekly submission review queue. Facilitator and Admin action reviews; CMs can confirm submissions are logged.

  1. 1Open Reviews. Submissions are grouped per week.
  2. 2Open a review card — submission details show inline.
  3. 3Log feedback against the correct week; toggle a week open/closed as needed.
Tip: Admins can copy a submission link from Reviews settings to share with participants.

6. Sessions & Attendance

Track sessions and their readiness, and let participants sign in without an account.

Readiness checklist

Each session record has an editable checklist — tick items off as you prepare.

Public sign-in/out

  1. 1Open the session and share its public attendance link (/attendance/…).
  2. 2Participants sign themselves in (and out) — no login required.
  3. 3Attendance flows back into the session record.

7. Community (CM Tracker)

Your weekly report row, CM-editable. File it at end of week (Fri):

  • Prompts posted · attendance & submissions updated
  • Silent count · stuck count · escalations raised
  • Energy level · key concerns · next actions
  • Set Status = Done and Weekly report sent = yes
  1. 1Open Community and use the create-report CTA.
  2. 2Pick the week from the dropdown — your CM name auto-fills.
  3. 3Fill the prompts accordion and participant pills, then save.

8. Tasks

Your work queue. Tasks are owned by role labels (e.g. "CM Owner") and appear for whoever holds that role.

  • Change status inline; completed tasks hide by default.
  • Use comments to say what's blocked — @mentions render as chips.
  • Don't let an overdue task sit silently; comment the blocker.
Note: Recurring duties (midweek check-in, deadline-day confirmation, end-of-week report) arrive here automatically and the system nudges you on them.

9. Safeguarding Escalations

Anything you can't resolve goes through the escalation system — grounded in the Safeguarding Policy — not a DM.

  1. 1Bump Escalations raised on your weekly Community row.
  2. 2Write the detail in Key concerns.
  3. 3It surfaces on the Dashboard for the core team — that's how you get leads involved.

10. Notifications & Reminders

  • The app sends web push notifications — allow them when installing the PWA.
  • A daily reminder runs at 08:00 for due/overdue tasks owned by your role.
  • The Alerts page collects in-app notifications.

11. Admin: Cohorts, Import, Export Admin

Import the workbook (one-time)

  1. 1Go to /admin/import.
  2. 2Type the confirmation phrase IMPORT_MORPH_OPS.
  3. 3Upload the workbook and map columns; records land in the right modules.

Export a backup

Visit /admin/export for a JSON backup. Do this regularly.

Cohorts, reviews & reminders

Configure cohorts, week labels, review open/close, and reminder timing from Settings.

Note: The first Supabase user to sign in when no active admin exists is auto-activated as admin (claim_first_admin()).

12. FAQ

I don't see a page I expected — why?
Pages are gated by role. CMs and Facilitators see everything but can only edit specific areas. If you genuinely need access, ask an admin.
I flagged a student Red but didn't make a task — did it work?
Yes. Setting Risk = Red auto-creates an outreach task on the owning CM. Check that CM's Tasks queue.
A participant's submission isn't showing in the right week.
Submissions land in the week bucket of the public submit link they used. Confirm they used the current week's link; admins can copy the correct link from Reviews settings.
I'm not getting reminders.
Install the app to your home screen and allow notifications. Reminders fire at 08:00 for tasks owned by your role.
Do participants need an account?
No. They submit work and sign into sessions through public links — no login required.
How do I back up the data?
Admins can export a JSON backup any time from /admin/export.
